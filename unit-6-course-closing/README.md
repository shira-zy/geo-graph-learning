# Unit 6 — Course Closing

A short (~30 min) **closing session**, delivered **once at the very end** of the
chapter, after Unit 5. It is the bookend to the Unit 0 orientation — the
before/after mirror of where the course started.

The session runs in three movements:

- **The arc** — one recap slide per unit (Units 1–5): the motivating question in
  past tense, what you can now do, and the one-line residue each unit leaves
  behind — then the slide that connects them into a single pipeline (the
  bus-archive thread from [`../SYLLABUS.md`](../SYLLABUS.md)).
- **Working with AI** — the North Star (DIRECT → CHOOSE → INTERPRET), the
  weekly direct → interpret → extend loop (see [`../rubric.md`](../rubric.md)),
  the "wrong-class" reflex, and how to carry this into building your *own*
  AI-assisted projects.
- **The mirror** — a re-ask of the Unit 0 pulse so we can see, together, the
  effect of what we did — the room in its own words.

## Materials

- `theory.pdf` / `theory.html` — the closing deck.

There is **no demo, practice, or homework** for this unit — the hands-on work
ended with Unit 5. This session is reflection and synthesis. See the
[course syllabus](../SYLLABUS.md) and the [rubric](../rubric.md).
